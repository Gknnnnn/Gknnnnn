## 🎓 Research Interests

My research operates at the intersection of **econometrics**, **energy economics**, and **environmental economics**, with a focus on emerging markets and the Turkish economy.

- **Environmental & Energy Economics** — Environmental Kuznets Curve (EKC), ecological footprint, renewable energy transitions, carbon emission dynamics
- **Climate-Growth Nexus** — CO₂ emissions, climate change policy, sustainable development (SDGs), Jevons Paradox
- **Migration-Carbon-Growth Nexus** — Cross-border migration, demographic shocks, and their macroeconomic-environmental interactions
- **Sustainable Agriculture** — Renewable energy adoption in Turkish agriculture, ecological productivity paradox
- **Political Economy of Eurasia & Turkey** — Eurasian Economic Union (EAEU), sovereign wealth funds, trade policy, external debt dynamics
- **Panel Econometrics** — Causality, cointegration, and dynamic modeling in heterogeneous panels

## 🔬 Econometric Toolkit

| Category | Methods |
|----------|---------|
| **Panel Data** | Fixed/Random Effects, Dynamic Panels (GMM-Sys, GMM-Diff), Hausman test |
| **Causality** | Dumitrescu-Hurlin, Konya Bootstrap, Toda-Yamamoto, Granger |

| **Time Series** | VAR, ARIMA, GARCH, unit root tests (ADF, PP, KPSS, Zivot-Andrews) |
| **Identification** | IV/2SLS, DiD, RDD, control function approach |
| **Cross-Sectional** | OLS, WLS, Quantile Regression, Spatial Econometrics |
